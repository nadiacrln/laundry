Our Location

Tembalang District, located in the city of Semarang, is known as a dynamic area with various supporting facilities, including various laundry services. With this Laundry WebGIS, you can easily:

  1. Find the Nearest Laundry Location
    Our interactive map helps you find the nearest laundry place with just a few clicks. All locations are equipped with complete information, such as address, contact number, and operating hours.
  2. Evaluating Service Options
    Find information about types of services, such as laundry by the kilo, express laundry, dry cleaning, or laundry pick-up and delivery services.
  3. Compare Prices and Reviews
    Access information about service rates and customer reviews to choose a laundry that fits your needs and budget.
  4. Easy Navigation
    Equipped with directions, you will have no trouble finding the laundry location, even if you are new to Tembalang District.
